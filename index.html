Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes! You can start learning for free with our beginner lessons. Some advanced courses may require a subscription, but the basic features are always free.
3. Do I need to create an account?
Creating an account is optional for basic practice. However, if you want to save your progress, unlock quizzes, and track your learning journey, we recommend signing up.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Our lessons include vocabulary lists, daily practice words, and interactive flashcards. You can also try the “Search” feature to learn the meaning and usage of any word instantly.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes! After completing certain structured courses, you’ll receive a digital certificate that you can download and share.
